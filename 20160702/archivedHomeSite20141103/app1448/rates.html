RATES

Like most contractors, I have a variety of rates and flat fees that I have negotiated over a variety of tasks and situations.

 

In short:

 

Let's talk about what you need done, and we'll set a rate that reflects the going rate for my skill level and relevant skill set.

 

In general:

 

Hourly rates on most short-term contracts vary from $30-80, depending on the skill sets required and the length of the contract. Lowest rates are generally given to non-profits and agencies in need. Top rates are assigned to contracts that require 24/7 presence, extensive or unplanned travel, and any troubleshooting job.

 

Hourly rates on long-term contracts are negotiable, and retainers are encouraged for contracts over six months in length.

 

Fixed rates may be applied to tasks with a fixed scope and timetable.

 

Day rates (days exceeding 10 hours work and/or travel) are available to provide a fixed cost to travel and overnight contract work.
