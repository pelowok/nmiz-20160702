SAMPLES  |  visual design

This Proof-of-Concept artboard sequence drives the user interface for an AXIS Communications trade show touch table sales tool. The tool allows client tracking linked to products and vendors, and incorporates third-party products as well. The tool links multiple vendors and products to single clients using an opt-in approach to sales.

 

The UI was designed to be used with the cooperation of the sales representatives alongside the clients, on a large tabletop touch screen.

Here are two specific-length videos I created for a windmill-technology company, SecondWind, for use in their trade booth.

 

The first is a proof-of-concept video. The request was to demonstrate the concept and movement of wind without using any of the tropes normally associated with the industry. For the POC, I pushed a transition effect that mimics the whirlwind eddies often seen in leaves and sand.

 

The second is timed call-to-action video that was part of a loop. The effects are generated through TrapCode and 3D text layering in AfterEffects.
